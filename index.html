A gentler approach to walking

Most fitness apps are designed to push you harder.

Walk Your Way was built around a different idea: movement should support your life, not control it.

Whether you live with chronic pain, burnout, disability, fatigue, anxiety, or simply want a calmer relationship with exercise, the app is designed to help you understand your own pace and capabilities.

No streaks. No punishment. No shame.

Features

Build walks that fit you

Create routes based on comfort level, time, energy, and walking preferences.

Gentle check-ins

Supportive daily check-ins focused on understanding how you feel — not pushing harder.

Accessibility-focused

Designed with chronic pain, disability, burnout, and sustainable movement in mind.

Weather-aware walking

Walking suggestions that consider weather, conditions, and comfort.

Walk without a route

Start a casual walk without needing to fully plan ahead.

Help support development

Walk Your Way is independently developed and intentionally designed to avoid manipulative engagement systems.

Patreon support helps keep the project sustainable while allowing the app to remain accessible.
